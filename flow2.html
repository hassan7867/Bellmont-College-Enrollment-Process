Bellmont College – Enrollment Lifecycle

This document presents a comparison between the current manual enrollment system and the proposed integrated solution at Bellmont College in partnership with Liverpool Hope University (LHU).

Current System Challenges vs Proposed Automated Solution

🚧 Key Challenges in Current System
  • 📊Manual Data Collection
    • 📥 Student information is collected manually through forms or email.
    • 🔁 Results in repeated data entry and poor data consistency.
  • 🗂️Fragmented File Sharing
    • 📦 Files are zipped and shared via Google Drive or email.
    • 📉 No tracking or visibility once shared externally.
  • 📉Inaccurate Attendance Tracking
    • 📝 Attendance taken on paper or spreadsheets.
    • 🔍 Discrepancies common due to human errors and delays.
  • 🔄No Real-Time Synchronization
    • 🧩 Bellmont SIS and LHU SITS function in isolation.
    • 📉 Delays and misalignment across systems.
  • 📪Lack of Communication Flow
    • 📬 Heavily dependent on back-and-forth emails.
    • ⏱️ Slows down response time and updates.
  • ⚠️GDPR Vulnerabilities
    • 🔓 Files shared on unsecured platforms.
    • 📂 No audit trail or permissions tracking.
  • ⏳Lack of Automation
    • 👩‍💻 All processes depend on human input.
    • 📉 Increased risk of delay, error, and burnout.

🧩 Final Summary: Current Enrollment Lifecycle is manual, error-prone, and unsustainable. Without integration, it burdens staff, delays student services, and poses compliance risks.

✅ Proposed Unified & Automated System
  • 🔗API Integration Between SIS & SITS
    • 📥 Enrollment API: Real-time sync of applications and registrations.
    • 📊 Assessment API: LHU marks are automatically reflected in SIS.
    • 📆 Attendance API: Seamless two-way attendance flow.
  • 🧭Centralized Student Lifecycle
    • 📎 Submit and track all records from a single dashboard.
    • 📁 No more manual uploads or scattered storage.
  • 👥Integrated Class Setup
    • 🔗 Teams links generated and distributed automatically.
    • 🎓 Students grouped directly via SIS.
  • 📅Digital Attendance
    • 🖐️ Marked via Desktop/mobile/tablet and synced instantly.
    • 📤 Sent to LHU without staff intervention.
  • 📚Assessment Tracking
    • 📝 View Moodle submissions directly in SIS.
    • 🔁 Track feedback and marks automatically.
  • ⏭️Automated Progression
    • 📈 Final marks trigger next-year enrollment instantly.
    • 📌 Saves staff time and ensures accurate outcomes.
  • 🔐GDPR Compliance & Security
    • 🛡️ Secure, encrypted systems with audit logs.
    • 🧾 Permission-based access to protect data.

🔗 Final Vision: A fully automated, secure, and GDPR-compliant system where every student interaction is synced in real time—empowering both staff and students with clarity, speed, and confidence.
